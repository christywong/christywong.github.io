Problem

Planning social events or hangouts with friends can be difficult, as the process can easily become disorganized. For example, in group chats, people send over multiple Yelp links, which tend to flood the chat window and can also get lost in the conversation. People need a way to save the places they want to go to with their friends in a simple and organized manner.

Our Solution

With Bucket, users will be able to collaboratively create and edit bucket lists with their different groups of friends. Our mobile-responsive web app is connected with the Yelp API, allowing users to directly search for places they wish to go to straight from our app. Users can create groups, where each group has their own page with all of their respective bucket lists on that page. Each list they create is categorized with the tag name, which organizes all their lists. Users can also archive places they have previously been to. Bucket makes planning future hangouts a hassle-free and stress-free process!
